Learn: Ethical Frameworks

Ethical theories help us analyze technology decisions. Each offers a different perspective:

  • Utilitarianism: Focuses on maximizing overall good.
  • Deontology: Emphasizes rules and duties.
  • Virtue Ethics: Focuses on character and integrity.
  • Social Contract Theory: Emphasizes fairness and cooperation.

Apply: Ethical Decision Scenario

A company collects user data without clear consent to improve its AI system. Which ethical framework BEST supports protecting user privacy?

Practice: Ethical Reasoning Process

Arrange the steps in the correct order:

Apply ethical framework
Identify the issue
Analyze consequences
Make decision
